About Us

We Are a Think Tank

Our employees and partners have diverse experiences and backgrounds. We work together, leveraging these unique experiences, to design a more efficient and sustainable version of our ecosystem.

Our goal is to fund collaborations inspired by complex problems - groups driven to build towards a utopian-ideal future for the greatest number of life-forms.

We have previously felt - without purpose, life feels meaning-less. But here, our purpose is straightforward: Evolving a model of our operating environment, and executing on steps (and goals) aligned with our long-term visions of the future.

As humanity develops, and we continue to create, institutional chaos and complexity will transform and persist. Our mission is to attract professionals with an insatiable intrinsic motivation to tackle collective problems that threaten our continued prosperity; continuously ensuring our impact on the globe is net-positive.
