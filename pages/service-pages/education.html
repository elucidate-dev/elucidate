Education

Interdisciplinary, small-group, education program.

Quasi-supervised deep learning, paired with student led lectures, and one to one tutoring.

As technology advances, the future of education will change. The variety resources and tools available to the modern student suggests that teachers can't possibly keep up with what methods are working for students.


Elucidate is developing an education program that combines self-led deep learning sessions with periodic teacher-led lectures. One to one mentoring, given small class sizes, will give students the opportunity to excel at their own pace; and, give educators the ability to tailor their approach to each individual student.
